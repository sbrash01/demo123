A five-piece band that reads the room

The Velvet Hours have played weddings, warehouse parties, and backyard shows across Los Angeles since 2016. Hazy guitars, big harmonies, and a rhythm section that does not quit, building a set around your crowd so everyone from your college roommates to your aunt ends up on the floor.

What every booking includes

  • Up to four hours of live music in two or three sets
  • A custom first-dance arrangement learned just for you
  • Professional PA, stage lighting, and a sound engineer
  • An emcee for toasts, introductions, and announcements
  • Playlist curation for dinner and cocktail hour between sets
