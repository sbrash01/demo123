Meet the band

Five musicians raised on the LA indie scene, from Silver Lake clubs to desert festivals. Between us we have played hundreds of weddings, parties, and club nights, and we still love every downbeat.

  • Mara Lane

    Lead Vocals

    A powerhouse voice who has fronted indie bands all over the LA scene. Mara reads a room in seconds and never misses a key change.

  • Desmond Cole

    Guitar & Vocals

    Reverb and jangle to the core. Desmond arranges every first dance the band plays and harmonizes on the choruses everyone knows.

  • Nadia Okafor

    Keys & Synth

    Conservatory-trained and endlessly versatile, Nadia layers the dreamy synths and piano lines that turn a good night into a great one.

  • Theo Vance

    Bass

    The pocket. Theo locks in with the drums to keep the floor moving from the first dinner set straight through the last call.

  • Marcus Reed

    Drums

    Twenty years behind the kit and a metronome for a heartbeat. Marcus sets the tempo for every celebration the band plays.
